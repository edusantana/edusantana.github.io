My first decision was to update myself about new versions of technologies. I joined [freecodecamp](http://freecodecamp.com/) last week, it was very basic staff but I enjoyed the bootstrap layout for responsive design. I'm doing my portfolio now.

After a week looking for companies that allows remote software developers, I found Toptal on stackoverflow and watched [this video](https://vimeo.com/130765227), after that, I decided that's where I want to be working...

And here I am, applying to them! Let's see what the future holds for me...
